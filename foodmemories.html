My Favorite Food Memories

This past summer, I lived right by Michigan Creamery, and I became a regular very quickly. Their allergen-free menu is more diverse than most, and this combo was surprisingly good.

This picture was taken mid-quarantine, when I decided to celebrate the end of a hectic week with Yo's Ultimate Chocolate Cake. In the process, I realized how therapeutic it was to set aside even just an hour to do something I enjoy.

I tried sushi for the first time last year! The Popcorn Lobster at Totoro, a special California roll topped with deep-fried lobster, is now one of my favorite meals.

Surprising my best friend at Mason Hall at midnight for her 20th birthday, with a cookie cake that read 'Bite Me'. She saw it coming, but it was still a great time.

I took this picture at Casa Dominick's to capture a great dinner in their outdoor seating space. The food was hearty, and the ambience was warm.

In this picture, I'd gotten together with two new friends from class to make lemon butter garlic chicken pasta. Dinner was delciious and I loved getting to know them better!

This was the first meal I cooked with my roommates in our first ever apartment. We were so proud of ourselves!

The classic chocolate cake at Dom Bakeries in Ypsi. Tastes best when your friends all grab a fork and dig in.

A homemade creamy Indian chicken curry by my best friend and I. We plated it up nicely and took pictures on a disposable effect camera app.
