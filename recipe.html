Lemon Garlic Pasta

Time: 30 minutes
Serves: 3

Ingredients

  • 8oz fettucine pasta
  • 2 lemons
  • 8-10 cloves of garlic
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Oregano
  • Dried parsley

Instructions

  1. 1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water according to package instructions. Set aside, reserving some pasta water for later≥
  2. 2. While your pasta is boiling, peel and mince your garlic. Dice your spring onions, and juice your lemons.
  3. 3. Add butter to a pan on medium heat. Once sizzling, add garlic and spring onions, stirring often.
  4. 4. Add lemon juice, along with pasta water for desired consistency and wait for sauce to come to a boil.
  5. 5. Now toss cooked pasta into sauce and coat well.
  6. 6. Garnish with salt, red pepper flakes, oregano and parsley to taste. Serve hot and enjoy!

10-minute Green Salad

Time: 10 minutes
Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 green bell pepers
  • 16 oz kale
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Garlic powder

Instructions

  1. 1. Chop your bell peppers.
  2. 2. To a large pan with hot oil, add garlic and fry until golden.
  3. 3. Add chopped bell peppers and stir. Meanwhile, destem and chop kale into bite-sized pieces.
  4. 4. Add kale and sauteé for a minute before turning off the heat.
  5. 6. Garnish with lemon juice and salt, red pepper flakes, and garlic powder to taste.

TikTok Garlic Chilli Oil Noodles

Time: 10 minutes
Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Shinyun Ramen
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 spring onions, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. 1. Set aside seasoning packet and cook ramen according to package instructions.
  2. 2. In a heat-safe bowl, add spring onions, garlic, ramen seasoning and red pepper flakes.
  3. 3. In a separate pan, heat olive oil.
  4. 4. Carefully add hot oil into seasoning bowl and mix well. Watch out for splattering.
  5. 5. Now toss in the cooked ramen and mix until evenly coated. There you have it - elevated instant ramen.
